**Biological considerations for the use of CRISPR Cas9 to edit genomes in animals**
*Justice* - concerns over the expenses and whether everyone will have access  
*Beneficence* - maximising benefits, reducing cat allergies for example  
*Non-maleficence* - ensuring that any editing does not harm animals  
*Integrity* - honest and unbiased reporting of all data and results of genome editing regardless of positive or negative outcomes
