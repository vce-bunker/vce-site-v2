**➡️ Positive feedback**

- only in females
- example childbirth- contractions of the uterus stimulate the **pituitary gland** to release oxytocin which triggers more contractions
___

➡️ **Negative feedback**
Stimulus- the initial change
Receptor- detects change
Modulator (processing centre)- releases hormones
Effector- produces a respons in response to the hormone

In negative feedback, response reduces the initial stimulus.
