**Overview**
dna- encoded string in cell
mRNA- a clone of a dna that is sent out of the nucleus (in eukaryotes)
tRNA- a thing that attaches to one amino acid and 3 bases, multiple of these to decode a string (gene) of dna into a string of amino acids
-rRNA- part of the ribosome
