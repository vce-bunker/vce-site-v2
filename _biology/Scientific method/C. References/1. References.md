Clear and accurate acknowledgement of sources and assistance

- Acknowledge sources of information and if required, use APA referencing style
- Referencing a Book:

The format is:          Authors; (date) Book title. Publisher: Place of publication.

*Example:*

*Kinnear J & Martin M.(2013) Nature of Biology Book Two. Wiley: Australia.*

- Referencing a website: Title of the page; (website address) date accessed

*Example:*

*DNA Fact Sheet (http://www.genome.gov/25520880) 7th November 2012.*

- In text referencing should also be used.

*Example*:

*DNA takes the form of a double helix (www.dna.com.au).*
