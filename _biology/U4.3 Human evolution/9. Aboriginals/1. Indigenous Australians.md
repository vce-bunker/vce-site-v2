**What are songlines and stories and how are they relevant to determining migratory routes and demonstrate connection to country**
1. Songlines are stories, art, dance and song passed through generations that identify and describe routes through Country to areas of importance
2. *act as oral history* depicting aboriginal  **knowledge of Country**, both **physical** **and spiritual connections**
3. and can match evidence from fossils and genetic testing to migration routes
4. Confirmed by evidence from the fossil records, such as dating fossils and artefacts.

this connection can help trace ancestor's routes/journeys through retelling stories and understanding of land and water passed down through generations
