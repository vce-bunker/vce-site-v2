With reference referring to one qualitative characteristic, explain the role of the physical count stocktake
Verifiability (& faithful representation)

THe role of a physical count is to verify the accuracy of the inventory cards by comparing them to the physical count so that the inventory reported in the blance sheet is free from bias, and in the process detect any iventory loss or gain. This ensures *that* *the information reported is a faithful representation of the real world economic event it represents, completely neutral(without bias) and free from error and* that **independent observers can reach a concensus that such information is faithfully represented.
