**Explain how the depreciation method is chosen and justify why**

The depreciation method chosen should match the asset’s revenue earning pattern.

|  | straight line method | reducing balance method |
| --- | --- | --- |
| assumes that the asset will | contribute evenly to revenue, doing the same job when it is old as when it is new. | contribute more to earning revenue at the start of its life and less as it ages |
| This method allocates | the same amount of depreciation expense in each reporting period | a higher depreciation expense at the beginning of the asset’s life than at the end |
| and is calculated as a percentage of the | historical cost of the asset | carrying value of the previous reporting period |
| Typically, this would be used for assets such as | office furniture and fixtures and fittings | equipment, vehicle, copier etc. |

Choosing the appropriate depreciation method fulfils Accural Basis as it ensures that the depreciation incurred is based on the asset’s revenue earning pattern, and is matched against the revenue it assisted in earning in order to determine an accurate net profit.
