**TRU Vigilantes Fight Crime**

Timeliness
Information is available to decision makers in time to be able to influence a decision, allow for problems to be identified sooner so that corrective action can be taken EARLIER.

Relevance
Information is capable of making a difference to the decision made by the users
(useful to decision making)

Understandability
Comprehensible to users with reasonable knowledge of business & economic activities.
Presented clearly & concisely with the use of tables, headings and totals

Verifiability
Supported by evidence- Retention of source documents, different knowledgeable and independent individuals can reach a consensus that info is faithfully represented (then talk about faithful rep)

Faithful representation
Complete, without bias & free from error, Faithful rep of the real world [economic event] it represents

Comparability
Use of consistent accounting methods so all periods will be same format so can compare performance
