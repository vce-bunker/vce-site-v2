Referring to one Qualitative Characteristic, explain the purpose of preparing an Accounts Receivable Schedule (3 marks)

The total of the Accounts payable/receivable schedule is calculated by adding together the balances of all the account payable/receivable records. If the transactions have been recorded correctly it should match the figure calculated using the Accounts payable/receivable formula. This double check ensures that the figure reported in the Balance Sheet provides a Faithful representation of the balance of Accounts payable, complete and free from error.
