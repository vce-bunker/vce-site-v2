|                                                |                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    |
| ---------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Assets                                         | Liabilities<br>+Owner's Equity                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     |
| Accounts Receivable                            | Opening Accounts receivable balance<br>+ credit fees including GST (sales journal)<br>- sales returns including GST (no record)<br>- receipts from accounts receivable (cash receipts journal)<br>- Discount expense (no record)<br>- Bad debts including GST (no record)                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          |
| Accounts Payable                               | Opening Accounts payable balance<br>+purchase of items on credit including GST (Purchases journal)<br>- payments to accounts payable (cash payments Journal)<br>- purchase returns (no journal)<br>- discount revenue (cash payments journal)                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      |
| GST Balance                                    | Opening Balance GST \| if GST receivable= a negative balance, if GST payable= positive opening balance<br>-GST settlement (Cash payments Journal under Sundries) OR + GST refund (Cash reciepts Journal, Sundries)<br>+GST received on cash sales (Cash receipts journal- GST column)<br>+GST charged on credit sales (Sales journal - GST column)<br>-GST on sales returns (additional info)<br>-GST paid (Cash payments Journal - GST column)<br>-GST incurred (Purchases Journal - GST column)<br>-GST on Bad debts (no record) |
| Cost of materials/supplies used                | Amount of supplies at start<br>+supplies purchased on cash<br>+supplies purchased on credit<br>-supplies remaining at end                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          |
| Adjusted gross profit                          | +Cash fees<br> +Credit fees<br> -Sales returns      _<br> Net sales<br> -cost of sales (subtract sales returns)<br> -other stuff required for getting the item to the shop (see accounting Y10T3)_____<br> Gross profit<br> -/+ inventory loss/gain         _<br> adjusted gross profit                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            |
| Depreciation expense (straight line method)    | (Historical cost - Residual value) / useful life                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   |
| Depreciation rate (straight line method)       | (Depreciation expense / Historical cost) * 100 = % (of NCA cost) per annum                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         |
| Depreciation expense (reducing balance method) | Carrying value * % rate                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            |
| Accounts receivable turnover                   | (Average Accounts Receivable / Net Credit Sales (plus GST)) * 365 = Avg number of days                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             |
| APTO                                           | (Average Accounts Payable / Net Credit Purchases (plus GST)) * 365 = Avg number of days                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            |
| ITO                                            | (Avg inventory/ Cost of goods sold) * 365 = Average number of days                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 |
| Net realisable value (NRV)                     | Estimated selling price less direct selling expenses                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               |
| Inventory write-down                           | Cost price less NRV                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                |
| Net profit margin                              | (Gross profit / net sales) * 100 = %                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               |
| Gross profit margin                            | (Net profit / Net sales) * 100 = %                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 |
| Cash flow cover                                | Net cash flows from operations / Avg current liabilities                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           |
| Return on assets                               | (net profit / average total assets) * 100 = %                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      |
| Asset turnover                                 | net sales / average total assets = times per period                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                |
| Return on owner's investment                   | (net profit / average capital) * 100 = %                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           |
| Working capital ratio                          | current assets / current liabilities = ratio (current assets : 1)                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  |
| Quick assets ratio                             | (current assets - (inventory + prepayments)) / current liabilities = ratio (quick assets : 1)                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      |
| Debt ratio                                     | (total liabilities / total assets) * 100 = %                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       |
