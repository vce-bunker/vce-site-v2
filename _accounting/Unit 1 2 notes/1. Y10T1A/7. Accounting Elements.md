Asset

_ was treated as a current/noncurrent asset. This is because it was a present economic resource controlled by the entity (as a result of [insert past event]) which has the potential to produce future economic benefits (cash) within the next/for more than 12 months of the end of the reporting period {when we receive the balance from the [ATO/other lender].

Liability
_ was treated as a current/non current liability. This is because it was a present obligation of the entity (as a result of [past event]) to transfer an economic resource (cash) and is expected to be settled within/after 12 months of the end of the reporting period when we pay the ATO/other lender.

Oe - the residual interest in the assets of the entity after the deduction of its liabilities.

Qaluities- claims on the assets of the firm (liabilities and oe)
