*chapter 3*

|                             | Meaning                                                                                                   | Advantages                                          | Disadvantages                       |
| --------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------- |
| Sole proprietorship         | A business owned by a single individual, operating their business in their own right under their own name | Cheap and easy to set up                            | Unlimited liability<br>Limited life |
| Partnership                 | 2-20 people working together                                                                              | Cheap to set up<br>More capital<br>                 | Unlimited liability<br>Limited life |
| Proprietary limited company | A registered legal entity with the right to do business in its own right                                  | Limited liability<br>Unlimited life                 | Expensive<br>Less capital           |
| Public company              | A large business structure that is also incorporated.                                                     | Limited liability<br>Unlimited life<br>More capital | Expensive                           |

note that past the first test you never need to now these. Not even in 34.
