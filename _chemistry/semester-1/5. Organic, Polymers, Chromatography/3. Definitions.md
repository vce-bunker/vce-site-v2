**homologous series**
a group of compounds that have identical functional groups and similar chemical properties but differ by a ch2 group in their structure

**structural isomers**
different variations of the structures that have the same molecular formula

**atom/functional group**
atom/group of atoms that give the molecule its physical/chemical properties

**Hydrocarbons**
alkanes, alkenes alkynes (hydrogen and carbon only)
- all hydrocarbons for 4 or less put gas.
- 4 or more for is liquid (for this test)

**Monomer**- single unit that can join together to form polymers  
**Polymer**- many monomers bonded together

**Dimer**- a complex where 2 molecules are linked together (can be both inter or intramolecular)
