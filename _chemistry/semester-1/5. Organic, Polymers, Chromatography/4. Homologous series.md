	-> prefix = # carbons, suffix = functional group
2. Side chains numbered based on lowest carbon
	 dash between number and word
	 multiple of same side chain - number, number-di___
3. multiple numbers - must be numerical order
	 side chains are alphabetical order, the di/tri/tetra are excluded when alphabetical ordering
