#### Schrodinger Model
Shell -> Subshell -> orbitals (2 electrons)\

**Notation**
$1s^22s^22p^63s^23p^63d^24s^2$
(Titanium)
- remember to put 3d before 4s

Short form
$[Ar]3d^2$
ar = the rightmost of row above
3d2 = last subshell filled

| Subshell | Electrons |
| -------- | --------- |
| s        | 2         |
| p        | 6         |
| d        | 10        |
| f        | 14        |

Exceptions: Chromium and Copper
