Atom
- smallest particle of a chemical that can exist

Element
- Substance made of 1 type of atom (pure)
- Monoatomic
	- made of 1 atom
- Diatomic
	- made of multiple atoms (can be different elements)

Molecule (eg $H_2O$)
- multiple nonmetals bonded together

Compound (eg $CH_4$)
- multiple different atoms bonded

Ions
- Different # of electrons

Isotopes
- Different # of neutrons

mixture  
- mix of multiple elements/compounds (but not bound together)

Atomic notation
## $$^{24}_{12}Mg^{2+}$$
24 = mass #
12 = atomic #
2+ = charge
